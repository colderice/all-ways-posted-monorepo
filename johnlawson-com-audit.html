Website Health Check

Check Status Finding
HTTPS / SSL Pass Site loads securely over HTTPS. Certificate is valid.
Title Tag Critical "John Lawson – Business Coach" — This is the wrong positioning. You are an AI Strategy Consultant and Futurist. Google is indexing and surfacing the wrong brand identity to every prospective client, event organizer, and media contact who searches your name.
Meta Description Critical Missing entirely. Google is auto-generating your search snippet from page body copy, which currently reads: "He is the brains behind the best-selling social marketing book..." — referencing a 10-year-old book, not your current AI work. You have zero control over how you appear in search results right now.
H1 Tag Pass One H1 on the homepage: "Empowering Entrepreneurs with Strategic Clarity." Single H1, correctly structured.
H2 Tags Fail All H2 tags on the homepage are empty strings. Section headings exist visually but have no text content inside the HTML tags — invisible to search engines.
Open Graph Title Critical OG Title is "Home 3" — a WordPress page template name that was never replaced. Every time your site gets shared on social media, the link preview title reads "Home 3." This is showing everywhere your URL is posted.
About Page Content Critical The /about/ page is filled with lorem ipsum placeholder text and is indexed by Google. The About Me page in the nav (/about-me/) has real content. The /about/ page does not — and Google is showing the lorem ipsum version in search results.
Privacy Policy Critical Contains unfilled template placeholders: "[Your Company Name]" and "[website/application/service]" are live in the policy text. Indexed by Google. Legal and trust exposure.
Schema Markup Warning Schema is present (Yoast SEO). However: (1) Person schema sameAs references http://www.JohnLawson.com — HTTP, not HTTPS. (2) Schema describes you as "Business Coach" not AI consultant. (3) Person and Organization types are combined on the same entity, which can confuse parsers.
robots.txt Warning Fully permissive — allows all crawlers to access all pages. The lorem ipsum /about/ page and unpopulated template pages are being freely crawled and indexed as a result.
Sitemap Pass Sitemap present with 11 sub-sitemaps (Yoast SEO). Last modified March 4, 2026. Properly referenced in robots.txt.
Contact Info on Homepage Fail No phone number or email visible on the homepage. Contact page has (678) 400-0580 — but this differs from the phone listed on Facebook: (888) 373-7612. NAP inconsistency.
Images Without Alt Text Warning 13 of 55 images on the homepage are missing alt text. Accessibility and SEO signal issue.
Blog Categories Warning All blog posts are filed under "Uncategorized." No topic taxonomy — misses internal linking and topical authority opportunities.
Social Links on Homepage Fail Homepage links only to LinkedIn — and three times. No links to Facebook, X (44.7K followers), Instagram, or YouTube. The footer has no social links at all.
