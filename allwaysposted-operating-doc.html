What This Business Is
All Ways Posted
A done-for-you social media content posting service targeting local service businesses and professional services directly, and marketing agencies via a white-label wholesale model. Content is created and delivered using Google's Pomelli AI as the fulfillment engine. We post. That's it. No claims about marketing outcomes, leads, or growth.
The Two Revenue Models
Model A — Direct to Business
Target: Local service businesses (salons, gyms) and professional services (realtors, dentists).

Entry offer: 2 weeks of free content built in Pomelli, delivered before asking for anything.

Price: $497/month · Month-to-month · Cancel anytime.
Model B — Agency Wholesale
Target: Small to mid-size agencies already billing for social but not consistently delivering it.

Model: White-label wholesale at $300–$600/client/month. Agency resells at $1,000+.

Promise: We fulfill under their brand. Clients never know we exist.
The Fulfillment Engine
Powered by Google Pomelli
labs.google.com/pomelli — Free in beta (US, Canada, Australia, New Zealand · English only). Scans client website, extracts Brand DNA (colors, fonts, tone), generates on-brand social posts automatically.

Key features: Instagram posts, Facebook ads, YouTube assets, email banners, animated video (Pomelli Animate via Veo 3.1), product photoshoot (launched Feb 2026 via Nano Banana 2). Natural language editing. No design skills required.

Phase 1 — New Client Onboarding (Day 1)
01
Payment Confirmed → Send Welcome
Reply to their email: "Payment received — you're in. Watch for your first batch Friday." Add to client tracker: name, business, website URL, start date, next delivery date, tier.
5 min
02
Run Pomelli — Build Brand DNA
Go to labs.google.com/pomelli. Enter client website URL. Let it extract Brand DNA: colors, fonts, tone of voice. Screenshot the profile. Note any gaps — if their site is weak, supplement manually before generating.
10 min
03
Generate First Batch + QC
Generate first 5 posts in Pomelli. QC check: Does it represent their business? Are colors/fonts on-brand? Is caption voice consistent? Make edits inside Pomelli before downloading. Download all assets to client folder.
10 min
Phase 2 — Weekly Delivery (Every Friday)
04
Generate Week's Content
Open Pomelli, load client session or re-enter URL. Generate 5 posts + 1 ad creative. Vary content types: promotional, educational, behind-the-scenes, testimonial, offer. Use Pomelli Animate on 1 post/week. Download and organize by client folder.
15–20 min
05
Deliver to Client
Drop files into shared Google Drive folder (one per client). Send short message: "Your content for the week is ready." Include one sentence on best posting times. Attach caption doc with copy-paste captions if needed.
5 min
06
Log and Track
Mark delivery date in client sheet. Note any feedback. Flag churn signals: no replies, late payment, complaints. Set next Friday reminder.
2 min
Phase 3 — Monthly Retention
07
Monthly Recap + Renewal
Send brief recap: "Here's what went out this month — 20 posts delivered." Include 1–2 engagement stats if the client shares them. Soft pitch add-on: "Want animated video posts next month? +$150/mo." Invoice sends same day every month.
10 min

Service Scope Disclaimer
Standard Disclaimer — Copy Into All Agreements and Invoices
All Ways Posted (allwaysposted.com) provides social media content creation and delivery only. This service does not include social media account management, paid advertising, community management, or marketing strategy of any kind. All Ways Posted makes no guarantees regarding follower growth, engagement rates, lead generation, website traffic, or revenue outcomes. Results from posted content depend entirely on factors outside the scope of this service, including but not limited to: offer quality, audience size, platform algorithms, and market conditions. The client is responsible for reviewing and approving all content prior to publication and for posting content to their own platforms unless a separate posting arrangement has been agreed upon in writing. Either party may cancel this service with 7 days written notice.
Agency Partner Agreement — Required Terms
White Label Terms
Agency may present deliverables under their own brand name. All Ways Posted name and branding will not appear on any deliverable. All Ways Posted will not contact agency clients directly under any circumstance. Agency owns the client relationship entirely.
Wholesale Pricing Terms
Agency pays All Ways Posted the agreed wholesale rate per active client per month. Agency may set their own resale price — All Ways Posted has no involvement in agency billing arrangements. Wholesale rates are billed monthly per active client.
Cancellation Terms
Either party may cancel any individual client engagement with 7 days written notice. No penalty for cancellation. Prepaid amounts for cancelled clients are not refunded. The full partnership agreement may be terminated with 30 days written notice.
Content Approval
Agency is responsible for reviewing and approving all content before delivery to their clients. All Ways Posted will revise content within 24 hours of a revision request. Agency assumes responsibility for any content forwarded to clients without review.
Scope Limitation
This service provides content creation and delivery only. All Ways Posted makes no guarantees regarding social media performance, follower growth, engagement rates, or business outcomes for agency clients. Agency is responsible for communicating this scope to their own clients.
